# LLM4Mol
LLM4Mol is a comprehensive repository dedicated to the collection and exploration of studies utilizing large language models for molecular design, protein research, and material science. This repository serves as a central hub for researchers, scientists, and enthusiasts interested in leveraging the power of language models for advancing our understanding and applications in these domains. Discover state-of-the-art techniques, novel approaches, and cutting-edge research papers that harness the potential of AI-powered language models in unraveling the complexities of molecules, proteins, and materials. Join our vibrant community and contribute to the exciting advancements in the field of LLM4Mol!
